Disney Castle Motion Graphic

Cinematic exploration of lighting, atmosphere, and timing in After Effects

After Effects Motion Design Animation Cinematic Lighting

Overview

This motion graphic was created in Adobe After Effects to explore cinematic lighting, atmosphere, and timing. The goal was to craft an immersive animated environment inspired by classic Disney visuals while practicing composition, motion pacing, and color design.

My Role

Motion Designer & Animator

Tools

Adobe After Effects, Illustrator

Focus Areas

Visual hierarchy, lighting, cinematic timing

Process & Exploration

I began with storyboarding key frames to establish pacing and mood. From there, I focused on building depth through layered lighting, particle effects, and smooth camera movement to create a magical, cinematic feel.

Color grading was critical — warm golden tones for the castle contrasted with cool night skies to evoke wonder and nostalgia. Timing was refined through multiple previews to ensure each transition felt intentional and emotionally resonant.

Final Result

The finished piece creates an atmospheric, dreamlike journey through a glowing Disney-inspired castle. It demonstrates strong control of motion, lighting, and composition to evoke emotion without dialogue.
