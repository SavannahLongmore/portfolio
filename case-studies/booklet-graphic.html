Survival Guide for Getting Remarried

Empathetic, humorous, and practical graphic booklet for navigating remarriage

InDesign Graphic Design Typography Print Layout

Overview

This personal project created a 20–24 page booklet offering gentle, practical, and occasionally humorous advice for individuals entering remarriage. It covers emotional preparation, blending families, legal considerations, communication, and self-care.

My Role

Graphic Designer & Author

Tools

InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop

Deliverable

Print-ready booklet (PDF)

Format

8.5 × 11 in, saddle-stitched

Design Approach

The tone needed to be warm, empathetic, and non-judgmental while avoiding clichés. I chose a clean, modern layout with generous white space, a soft neutral + pastel color palette, elegant serif headings, and highly readable sans-serif body text.

Key Design Choices

  • Two-column layout for readability and balance between text and breathing room
  • Custom illustrations and hand-lettered quotes for emotional warmth
  • Subtle icons and dividers to break up sections without clutter
  • High contrast and large type for accessibility (especially for older readers)

Final Result

The finished booklet feels approachable, thoughtful, and genuinely helpful. It balances checklists and practical advice with emotional encouragement, making a potentially sensitive topic feel manageable and hopeful.

Printed as a saddle-stitched booklet, it serves as both a personal resource and a meaningful gift for those entering remarriage.
