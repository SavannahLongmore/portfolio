Hi, I'm Savannah

I'm a multidisciplinary designer who loves solving real user problems through thoughtful research, clean interfaces, and a bit of motion magic.

My work blends UX/UI design, front-end development, graphic design, and motion — always with accessibility, clarity, and human connection in mind.
